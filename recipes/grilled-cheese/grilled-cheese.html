Grilled Cheese

Grilled cheese is a classic recipe, known for its cheesy and crunchy delicousness as well as its simplicity to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 slice of processed american cheddar cheese
  • butter

Directions

  1. Preheat a pan to medium high temprature.
  2. While the pan heats generously butter one side of one of your bread slices.
  3. Once pan is heated, place buttered bread butter side down onto the pan.
  4. Place slice of cheese on top of the bread on the pan.
  5. Butter one side of the bread not in the pan, place it on top of the cheese butter side up.
  6. Flip the sandwich and allow the other side to be browned
  7. Once browned take out of the pan and serve. Both sides of the sandwich should be golden brown.
