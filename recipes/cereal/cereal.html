Cereal

Cereal is an incredibly delicous and sugary breakfast and snack that can be enjoyed quickly and at any time!

Ingredients

  • A cereal brand of your choosing
  • milk, preferably whole milk

Directions

  1. Get a small bowl and place inside your desired amount of brand cereal.
  2. Pour in the milk just a little less than the height of the cereal that your poured in.
  3. Eat with a spoon and enjoy!
