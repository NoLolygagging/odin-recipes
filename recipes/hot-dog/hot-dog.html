Hot Dog

Hot dogs are an easy to make lunch item that will brighten up any day!

Ingredients

  • A fully cooked hot dog from the grocery store
  • A hot dog bun
  • Any condiment you like. For this recipe it is mustard

Directions

  1. Put hot dog in microwave for 1 minute.
  2. Once finished, put hot dog inside hot dog bun.
  3. pour mustard on top (or add any other condiment you like. common ones are ketchup and relish).
  4. Enjoy!
